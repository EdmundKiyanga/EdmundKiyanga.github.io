About Dr. Vincent Bernard Kihiyo

Dr. Vincent B Kihiyo was born in 1954. He holds a PhD in Forestry, from Sokoine University of Agriculture, and is an Associate Professor at the University. He is an expert on Natural Resources Conservation, Political Economy of Natural Resources, Governance and Anti-corruption Prevention Methods. He is currently the Director of Research, Control and Statistics in the Prevention and Combating of Corruption Bureau (PCCB) since 2000. He has vast experience in Research and Teaching at the University, and has attended numerous training and seminars, both within and outside the country on natural resource use and governance. Some of the Universities attended and visited include: Agricultural University of Norway at AAs, Indiana University at Bloomington, University of East Anglia, School of Development Studies, England and others. He has done commissioned consultancies on governance and conservation of Natural Resources.
