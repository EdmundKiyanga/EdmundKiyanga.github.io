About Mrs. Theresia S. Jackson

Mrs. Theresia S. Jackson was born in 1980. She holds an Advanced Diploma in Social Work, 2003, from the Institute of Social Work, Kijitonyama, Dar-es-Salaam. She has worked for the International Rescue Commission (IRC) as a Sexual and Gender Based Violence (SGBV) Officer at Kanembwa refugee camp in Kibondo district, Kigoma. She has also worked for the United Nations High Commission for Refugees (UNHCR) as a Field/Repatriation Officer in Kigoma. As a SGBV Officer with IRC her main duty was to ensure safety of female refugees in the camp against sexual and gender based violence from fellow refugees and camp officers by promoting good governance in the camp. As an UNHCR Repatriation Officer her duties were to screen new arrivals from the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC) and Burundi, escort those who have been repatriated from Tanzania to DRC and hand them over to relevant authorities in DRC in order to promote peace in Tanzania, Burundi, DRC and the region as a whole.
