Mr. Jesse Bahati Mashimi



About Mr. Jesse Bahati Mashimi

Mr. Jesse B. Mashimi was born in October 1948. He holds an MSc in Management Science and Computer Applications in Management Systems, 1995, from Cranfield University, UK; a BSc in Mathematics and Economics, 1973, from Dar-es-Salaam University; the National Computing Center (NCC) Higher Certificate in Systems Analysis and Design, 1976, UK; a Certificate in Management and Technology of Mechanical Wood Industries, 1981, from Helsinki, Finland; and a Certificate in Management Consulting Skills, 1986, from the Institute of Training and Organization Development (ITOD), Pittsburgh University, USA. He has been a lecturer at IDM and IFM in M.I.S., a Senior Economist in TWICO and Management Services Manager in Air Tanzania. He has taught as a part time lecturer in M.I.S. at the Dar-es-Salaam University and CBE. Since 1999 he is an Associate Consultant with the Tanzania Public Service College (TPSC) in the President’s Office, Public Service Management (PO-PSM), dealing with training of public servants and leaders on Leadership, Ethics, Good Governance and Anti-corruption, and staff of the Prevention and Combating Corruption Bureau (PCCB) in basic and intermediate investigation training programs. He has published papers in wood industry development in Tanzania and on public policy. He has also written a book on ‘Leadership and Good Governance and Development of Africa’ in Kiswahili titled, “Uongozi na Utawala Bora na Maendeleo ya Afrika”, published by TPSC, 2006.
